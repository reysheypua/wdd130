Dry Oar Rafting

Our Expert Guides

The team at Dry Oar Rafting is made up of skilled and passionate guides dedicated to creating safe, exciting, and unforgettable river adventures. Each staff member is trained in water safety and first aid, bringing years of experience and a love for the outdoors to every trip. From planning your journey to guiding you through the rapids, our team works together to make sure every moment on the river is fun, safe, and memorable.

History

Dry Oar Rafting began with a small group of outdoor enthusiasts who shared a love for adventure and the thrill of the river. What started as weekend rafting trips among friends soon turned into a mission to share that same excitement with others. With just a few rafts and a lot of passion, the team started guiding their first tours, focusing on safety, teamwork, and unforgettable experiences.

Over the years, the company has grown into one of the most trusted rafting outfitters in the region. Thousands of guests have joined our trips, from families seeking fun to thrill-seekers chasing the next big wave. Even as we’ve expanded, our goal remains the same — to help people connect with nature, discover their courage, and create lasting memories on the water.
